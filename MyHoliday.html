My Holiday Experience

Hello, my name is Viona Pandiangan. I want to tell you about this long 4-month holiday. During this holiday, I went home to my parents' house, located at RT.11/RW.3, Pasir Putih, Batang Peranap, INDRAGIRI HULU KAB, RIAU, ID 29356.

For the past 4 months, I have been working on an oil palm plantation with my parents. My tasks include cleaning the grass around each oil palm tree, collecting palm oil seeds that have been harvested, applying fertilizer, and gathering the oil palm leaves that have been pruned.

The experience I gained during this long holiday allowed me to understand the stages of caring for oil palm trees, from cleaning each tree, harvesting seeds, applying fertilizer, to selling the produce to agents. Through this, I was able to earn money to cover my tuition fees.

This picture was taken during my holiday, and it represents a moment of relaxation amidst the work on the plantation.
